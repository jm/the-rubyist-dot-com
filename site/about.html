About Us

Meet the folks behind the Rubyist

Jeremy McAnally

Editor in Chief

jeremy@therubyist.com

Jeremy has been working with Ruby since 2005, and is a Rails Core Contributor. He joined ENTP in mid-2008, where he now works on customer applications and training.

Jeremy spends his free time working on Open Source projects, including dcov, vintage, fleet and pillow. He is a co-author of Ruby in Practice and author of the self-published Humble Little Ruby Book. He lives with his wife in Huntsville, AL.

Pat Eyler

Assistant Editor

pat@therubyist.com

Pat Eyler is an Infrastructure Engineer for the LDS Church. He writes for O'Reilly and LinuxJournal.com, and organizes the Ruby Central Google Summer of Code program each year.

Pat also works with Mike Moore to organize the MountainWest RubyConf each year. In his free time, Pat enjoys reading, cooking, spending time with his family, and helping to build the Ruby community. He lives with his family in Salt Lake City, UT.

Leah Silber

Operations Editor

leah@therubyist.com

Leah has been involved with the Ruby community since 2007, and accepted a position running Community Relations at Engine Yard in January of 2008.

In addition to working on the Rubyist, Leah helps organize Regional Ruby Conferences around the world. She previously served as an editor of VisualjQuery Magazine and spends her free time editing technical books and articles. She lives with her husband in San Francisco, CA.
