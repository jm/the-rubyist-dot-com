What is the Rubyist?

The Rubyist is a technical magazine focused on Ruby, Rails, Merb, and anything else that surrounds the programming language that we all know and love.

Copies of the Rubyist are available in PDF form for $3 and print form for $8.

How often do you publish?

Our publication schedule is still being determined. In the future we may go monthly or quarterly; we're very early in our development so we're still trying to find our groove.

Back issues are always available from the purchasing site.

How can I get involved?

We are actively looking for authors. Please send any article proposals (or finished articles) to write@therubyist.com. Please note that we require a 30-day exclusive on the article, at which point it will be published on our website (under the Creative Commons License at your request).

We are also looking for advertisers. To inquire about rates, placement, and other prospectus-related information, please e-mail ads@therubyist.com.
